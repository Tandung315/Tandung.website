NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ Jiaxin


1. Giới thiệu

Jiaxin là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Gauntlets và sử dụng nguyên tố Aero .
Jiyan có thể được sử dụng như một DPS , Tank hay là một Support

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - Use these whenever possible to fill your Forte gauge quickly.

Heavy Attack - Also used to fill your Forte, but can be better than a Basic Attack if you have time to do so.

Resonance Skills - You can press the button (E) and it will do an attack that shoots a wave of air to damage and generate Forte and Resonance Energy, or you can hold it down and if anything attacks you in the next few seconds it will mitigate the damage of that attack, Parry if possible, then perform the attack. This has a lengthy aftercast, so it can be useful to save your Echo skill to cancel the animation after the strike.


Resonance Liberation- Creates a large wind field that pulls enemies towards the center while damaging them. After a few seconds, the wind will disappear and a massive explosion will occur. This is great to hold your enemies still so you can also charge up your Forte Heavy Attack on them for two pulsing damaging areas, or you can cast it and then immediately swap characters so your other team members can take advantage of the enemies being clumped together.


Rotation:
Heavy/Basic Attacks > E > Q > Heavy/Basic Attacks

Forte Heavy Attack + R > Outro

Outro – Provides a 38% Resonance Liberation buff to the next character for 14 seconds or until switched again.

Intro – Pull in nearby enemies while damaging them.

Echo Skils: Lingering Tunes – Mech Abomination - Instantly increase ATK by 12% for 15 seconds. For a soloist playstyle that stays on the field longer.

Echo Skils: Rejuvenating Glow – Bell-Borne Geochelone – For a more supportive playstyle and the 50% reduction from the Echo Skill can stack with the 50% reduction from her Forte to give 100% reduction for three hits. Use the Originite: Type IV gloves to trigger the buff more often.
