NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ Jiyan


1. Giới thiệu

Jiyan là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Broadblade và sử dụng nguyên tố Aero .
Jiyan có thể được sử dụng như một DPS có sát thương vô cùng cao trong những nhân vật 5 sao.

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - These will charge up his Forte, but Jiyan prefers to use Heavy Attacks.

Heavy Attack - Holding briefly and releasing will do a flurry of attacks that deal damage and increase his Forte gauge which has two bars to fill. Holding this longer will cause him to knock the enemy into the air, enabling him to follow up with an Aerial Attack.

Aerial Attack - After jumping into the air, Jiyan’s Aerial Attack will charge diagonally toward the enemy to smash the ground and then allow a Basic Attack to follow for more damage. If Jiyan knocked the enemy into the air using his Heavy Attack, then his Aerial will first knock them to the ground before you can charge at them.


Resonance Skills - Jiyan charges at the enemy dealing damage to any others he passes through. This can also be used while in the air to charge horizontally through the air. Consuming half of his Forte gauge will increase the damage of this skill by 20%. Don’t use this skill if it will prevent you from using your Ultimate with half of his Forte.


Resonance Liberation- If you have at least one bar of Forte, Jiyan will do a massive burst of damage to enemies in front of him. Then, regardless of his Forte, he will become empowered for 10 seconds to improve his resistance to interruption; and his Basic Attacks will have larger range, increased damage, be faster, and pull enemies in. These Basic Attacks will count as Heavy Attacks for the sake of stats and effects. His Resonance Skill will also cost no Forte and always deal the extra damage.


Rotation:
Resonance Skill > Heavy Attack > Aerial Attack > Basic Attack

Echo Skill > Resonance Liberation when above 30 Forte > Resonance Skill + Basic Attack until Ult ends.

Outro – The next character can use Heavy Attacks to trigger an extra burst from Jiyan’s dragon for the next 8 seconds, this can trigger up to two times.

Intro – Inherent Skills make his Intro grant 10% more ATK for 15 seconds.

Sequence 1 – Jiyan’s Resonance Skill can be used twice and the cost is decreased by 15 of his Forte gauge.

Sequence 2 – After using an Intro Skill, he gains 30 of his Forte gauge and his ATK is increased by 28% for 15 seconds.

Sequence 3 – When casting his Resonance Skill, Resonance Liberation, or Intro Skill, Jiyan gains 16% Crit. Rate and 32% Crit. DMG for 8 seconds.

Sequence 4 – After casting Resonance Liberation skills, the entire team gains 25% increase on Heavy Attack damage for 30 seconds

Sequence 5 – His Outro skill deals 120% increased damage and when he hits a target with any attack he gains a stacking buff of 3% ATK for 8 seconds, up to 15 stacks for a total of 45% ATK. This instantly reaches 15 stacks after using his Intro Skill.

Sequence 6 - Every time a Heavy Attack, Intro Skill, or Resonance Skill is used he will gain a stack of “Momentum.” This stacks up to two times. If the Resonance Liberation is cast when above 1 bar of Forte, it consumes all stacks and increases the initial damage by 120% for each stack.
