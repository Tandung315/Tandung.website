NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ CAMELLYA


1. Giới thiệu

Camellya là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Sword và sử dụng nguyên tố Havoc .
Camellya có thể được sử dụng như một DPS có sát thương AoE vô cùng cao trong những nhân vật 5 sao.

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - A five attack chain which can be held after the third part of the chain to continuously spin, dealing slightly more damage.

Resonace Skills - Deal damage then enter Blossom Mode which attaches Camellya to vines, allowing her to perform extra attacks while suspended in the air. Perform the same Basic Attack chain combo while in Blossom Mode and hold it to spin for a while until you release the button.
Attacks are stronger while in Blossom Mode and avoid ground attacks, but will cost extra Stamina and limit your mobility. You can dodge while suspended in Blossom Mode to move and evade attacks at the cost of Stamina. Camellya will remain in this stance even through character swaps. Use the Resonance Skill again or Jump to leave Blossom Mode.

Concerto Resonance - When at full Concerto Energy, the Resonance Skill will light up and using it will consume your Concerto to deal a heavy damaging attack while entering Camellya into Budding Mode. This gives her other attacks a 50% damage bonus.


Forte Circuit – Using Basic Attacks or the spinning attack will consume the Crimson Pistil Forte to grant extra Concerto Energy and Resonance Energy. The Forte resource is gained by using her Intro or Concerto Resonance skill and each grants the maximum of 100 stacks of Forte. When consuming these stacks, 10 stacks will translate into 1 stack of Crimson Bud. Crimson Buds do nothing until using the Concerto Resonance Skill which will convert the stacks into an extra 5% damage bonus.
Gain the Crimson Bud stacks by entering with her Intro

Resonace Liberation- Hits for heavy damage and grants 20 Concerto Energy


Rotation:
Intro > Basic Attacks until Full Concerto/10 Crimson Bud

Concerto Resonance > Resonance Liberation > Basic Attacks until Full Concerto

Outro – Deals heavy damage and will do even more if her Concerto Resonance has been used this swap.
