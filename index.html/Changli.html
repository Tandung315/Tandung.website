NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ CHANGLI


1. Giới thiệu

Changli là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Sword và sử dụng nguyên tố Fusion .
Changli có thể được sử dụng như một DPS có sát thương AoE vô cùng cao trong những nhân vật 5 sao và có lối chơi đa dạng bậc nhất.

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - A chain of four attacks. After the fourth attack, enter True Sight mode for 12 seconds. This attack chain can be performed while in the air, allowing you to float.

Resonace Skills - This skill has two charges that can be used with a low cooldown of 12 seconds. Dash towards the enemy and afterwards perform a ground slam and enter True Sight mode for 12 seconds. The initial dash is great for Parrying.

Heavy Attack - Perform an upward attack that will skip you through the Basic Attack chain to the third part. Try to do two basic attacks after this to gain True Sight.
Look at the buff above your health bar to see when you have True Sight.

Forte Circuit – At 4 stacks of Enflamement, your Heavy Attack will consume all stacks to perform a strong attack in a large radius around you, dealing Resonance damage. Receive 40% less damage during this somewhat long and vulnerable animation.

Aerial/Plunge Attack- Changli can perform her Basic Attack chain while floating in the air, but only one chain before a True Sight attack. Holding down the Left Mouse button while in the air will perform a Plunging Attack which will also skip to the third part of the Basic Attack chain. Try to do two basic attacks after this to gain True Sight.

True Sight– While in True Sight mode, you can perform a Jump (Space Bar), Aerial Attack, or Ground Basic Attack to dash at the enemy for Resonance Skill damage, ending True Sight and giving 1 stack of Enflamement (her Forte resource) up to 4 stacks. This attack also slows time.

Which attack you choose to do in True Sight will change depending on how you want to react to the situation. Using a Ground Basic Attack with True Sight will just lunge forward to attack, while Jumping or an Aerial Attack will soar you into the air which can provide vertical movement to avoid ground attacks or allow you to do aerial attacks.

Enflamement can be gained
1 – True Sight > Jump

1 – True Sight > Basic Attack

4 – Resonance Liberation

Resonance Liberation - Deals a large amount of damage to nearby enemies, gains four stacks of Enflamement, and will cause her next Forte Heavy Attack within 10 seconds to give her an additional 25% ATK.

Rotations
Attack Chain – 4 Basic Attacks > True Sight

Intro – True Sight > Plunge > 2 Basic > True Sight

Dodge – Dodge Counter > 2 Basic Attacks > True Sight

Aerial Plunge – Jump > Heavy Attack > 2 Basic Attacks > True Sight

Outro – The next character has their Fusion damage increased by 20% and Resonance Liberation damage increased by 25% for 10 seconds or until swapped out.
