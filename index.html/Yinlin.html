NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ YINLIN


1. Giới thiệu

Yinlin là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Rectifier và sử dụng nguyên tố Electro .
Yinlin có thể được sử dụng như một DPS hoặc là một Sup DPS .

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - Her chain of four Basic Attacks has a medium range and can hit multiple targets that are adjacent to each other, but it isn’t great for spread out enemies.

Heavy Attack - Spins Zapstring around her, hitting enemies in a wide radius. This is preferable to use over her Basic Attacks only in situations where enemies are more spread out. This can also apply Sinner’s Mark to as many targets as you can hit.

Resonace Skills - Zapstring hits enemies for a decent amount of damage and Yinlin enters Execution Mode for several seconds. While in this mode, Basic Attacks and Dodge Counter will cause a lightning strike to affected targets, with a maximum of four lightning strikes occurring. Afterwards, use the Resonance Skill again to finish the attack before it becomes unavailable for another significant amount of damage and to trigger her Inherent Skill which gives an ATK boost for 4 seconds.

The first and second Resonance Skill and the four lightning strikes in between will all generate a significant amount of Forte gauge, so you should always prioritize using this skill first, and use the second E as soon as you’ve hit the four lightning strikes. The second E attack will cancel the previous animation, so it is ideal to use it right after the third or fourth basic attack lands to remove the long aftercasts.


Forte Circuit – The Forte gauge can be gained by using Basic Attacks and Heavy Attacks depending on how many targets are hit. Use the Heavy Attack to hit targets who are spread out and the Basic Attack to hit targets more gathered together for faster Forte. The four Basic Attacks after using the Resonance Skill are the best way to gain Forte fast.

When at full Forte, Yinlin can use her Heavy Attack to turn the Sinner’s Mark on any enemies into a Punishment Mark. This will last 18 seconds and once per second enemies with the Punishment Mark will suffer an extra Electro strike when they take damage. You can swap to other characters who can still trigger this mark, and all enemies who have the Punishment Mark will take the extra strike if only one takes damage, so focus on hitting at least one target per second to maximize this damage.


Resonace Liberation- Triggers several lightning strikes for a massive amount of damage in a large radius. This also applies Sinner’s Mark which allows for a fast Forte Heavy Attack to get Punishment Mark on multiple targets.


Rotation:
Intro > Resonance Skill > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Forte Heavy > Swap

Resonance Liberation > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Forte Heavy > Outro

Intro – Deals damage and generates a considerable amount of Forte.

Outro – Leaves behind a fire field that deals damage over a few seconds.
