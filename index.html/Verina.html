NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ VERINA


1. Giới thiệu

Verina là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Rectifier và sử dụng nguyên tố Spectro .
Encore có thể được sử dụng như một Support có khả năng hồi phục cao

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - Her five part ranged Basic Attack chain will give one bar of Forte but mostly will be used to generate Concerto.

Heavy/Aerial Attack - One bar of Forte will be consumed when using a Heavy Attack or an Aerial Attack. Each bar consumed will heal the entire team and give Concerto.

Resonace Skills - Creates foliage in a line to damage enemies. Provides one Forte bar and 30 Concerto.


Forte Circuit – There are four bars of Forte with multiple ways of obtaining one each.

Resonace Liberation- Heals all characters and then adds a debuff to the enemy for 12 seconds which damages them and heals your current character when they hit the marked targets. It also provides 20 Concerto.


Rotation:
E > Forte Heavy/Aerial > Swap Character

Swap back to Verina after at least 12 seconds

E > Forte Heavy/Aerial > R > Q > Outro

Intro – Gives one Forte bar.

Outro – Heals the next character by 19% of Verina’s ATK per second for 6 seconds. All characters deal 15% more damage for 30 seconds.
