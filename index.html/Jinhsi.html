NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ Jinhsi


1. Giới thiệu

Jinhsi là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Broadblade và sử dụng nguyên tố Spectro .
Jinhsi có thể được sử dụng như một DPS có sát thương vô cùng cao trong những nhân vật 5 sao và có đội hình đa dạng nhất.

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic Attack - Four chain of attacks. After completing the full chain, the Resonance Skill will glow and allow you to enter the Incarnation state.

Resonace Skills - There are multiple variants of the Resonance Skill that have a different effect depending on the state of Jinhsi or your skill combos.

Normal Resonance – Charge toward the target and swing your sword. Only use this to engage on an enemy, but since it’s so low cooldown this is fine to use it this way.

Incarnation – After using four Basic Attacks, you can cast the Resonance Skill within the next 5 seconds to charge forward with a flourish of blades and enter the Incarnation state which will allow Jinhsi to float and perform new Basic Attacks and Resonance Skill.

Incarnation Resonance – While in the Incarnation state, her Resonance Skill will charge forward while in the air and summon a lightning strike.

Final Resonance – While in the Incarnation state, using the entire Four Basic Attack chain will enable a cutscene where a dragon unleashes a powerful dragon beam. Using this attack will enable your Outro/Intro skill character swap for free without using any Concerto once every 25 seconds. Damage will scale off of your Forte gauge.

Forte Gauge - Whenever any party member deals damage, increase your Forte by by 1. This can trigger once every three seconds per attribute, meaning a diverse composition with more than just Spectro damage sources will generate more Forte for Jinshi.
Also gain 2 Forte whenever a team member deals damage with a Coordinated Attack (damage dealt while that character is not the active character). This can trigger once every three seconds per attribute


Resonance Liberation - Deals an immense amount of damage to enemies in the area.

Rotations
Four Basic Attacks (Skip if Intro is used) > Echo Skill > Resonance Skill > Four Basic Attacks > Resonance Liberation > Resonance Skill > Outro

Use Coordinated Attacks on other characters > Swap to Jinhsi > Repeat

Intro – Allows the use of the Resonance Skill to enter Incarnation state without using the entire Basic Attack chain.

Outro – Increases the amount of Forte generated by allies for 20 seconds by reducing the internal cooldown between each trigger to 1 second instead of 3.
