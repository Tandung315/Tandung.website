NHỮNG HƯỚNG DẪN CƠ BẢN VỀ ENCORE


1. Giới thiệu

Encore là nhân vật 5 sao sử dụng Rectifier và sử dụng nguyên tố Fusion .
Encore có thể được sử dụng như một DPS có sát thương đơn mục tiêu vô cùng cao trong những nhân vật 5 sao.

2. Phong cách chơi

Basic\Heavy Attack - Ranged attacks mostly used to fill your Forte bar.

Resonace Skills - Shoots a flurry of attacks at the target and provides 15 Resonance Energy. Near the end of the animation this can be recasted to do a final blast, cast window shown by the white circle that appears. Prioritize using this skill whenever it is off cooldown
This leaves you vulnerable while casting it, but the first portion of the attack will continue even after swapping characters. If you swap back to Encore before the skill finishes casting, you can still complete the second cast while using another character and keeping Encore safe during her cast time.


Forte Circuit – Filled by using any of Encore’s attacks and even faster on Intro. Use Heavy Attack when the gauge is full to perform a long animation that will deal significant damage to enemies after a few seconds. While in this animation she takes 70% less damage, but she is still easily interruptible. Try to swap characters at the beginning of this cast as it will continue while she is off-field.


Resonace Liberation- Enters into the Cosmos Rampage state for 10 seconds, enhancing her Basic Attack, Dodge Counter, Heavy Attack, and Resonance Skill. Mostly use the Basic Attack and Resonance Skill as they will have faster cast times, less cooldowns, and do more damage.
Using the Forte Heavy Attack during this state will deal extra damage but end the Ultimate state. Save this for near the end when the Forte bar starts to blink.


Rotation:
Intro > Resonance Skill > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Forte Heavy > Swap

Resonance Liberation > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Resonance Skill > Basic Attacks > Forte Heavy > Outro

Intro – Deals damage and generates a considerable amount of Forte.

Outro – Leaves behind a fire field that deals damage over a few seconds.
